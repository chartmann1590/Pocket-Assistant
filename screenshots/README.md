# Screenshots

PNG assets for documentation, demos, and QA context.

- **`device/`** — Hand-captured device or emulator screens (home, item detail, settings, etc.).
- **`automation/`** — Frames saved during automated or manual test runs; filenames reflect the step or scenario.

Do not move launcher mipmaps or in-app drawables here; those stay under `app/src/main/res/`.
