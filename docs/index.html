Powerful features, zero compromise

Everything runs on your device. Your data never leaves your phone unless you choose to connect an Ollama server.

📷

Smart OCR Import

Snap a photo, share a screenshot, or import a PDF. ML Kit extracts text instantly with support for up to 5 PDF pages.

🤖

On-Device LLM

Choose from three downloadable models (Qwen3, Qwen2.5, Gemma 3n) running entirely on-device via LiteRT. No internet needed.

🔍

Neural Semantic Search

Find items by meaning, not just keywords. MediaPipe Universal Sentence Encoder powers natural language queries across all your items.

💬

RAG Assistant

Ask questions about your saved items. The assistant uses semantic search to find relevant context and generates informed answers.

✅

Tasks & Reminders

AI automatically extracts actionable tasks, due dates, and appointments. Set local reminders that work offline.

🔒

Privacy by Design

All core features work offline. No vendor backend, no data collection. Optional Ollama integration for more powerful models when you want it.

How it works

From capture to insight in seconds, entirely on your device.

1

Capture

Share a screenshot, photo, or PDF from any app, or paste text directly. OCR extracts text automatically.

2

Analyze

On-device AI categorizes the content, extracts entities like dates, amounts, and contacts, and generates a summary.

3

Organize

Items are automatically sorted into Bills, Messages, Appointments, and Notes. Tasks and reminders are created from the content.

4

Ask

Use the AI assistant to query your saved items. "When is my next bill due?" gets answered using your actual data.

Your data stays yours

Pocket Assistant processes everything locally using on-device ML models. There is no cloud backend, no telemetry, and no data leaves your phone. You are in full control.
