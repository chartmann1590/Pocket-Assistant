# Content rating (IARC) — answers

Use these when filling out **App content → Content ratings** in Play Console.
The app is a productivity tool with no violence, sexual content, or chat
between users. Expected result: **Everyone / PEGI 3**.

## Category

Productivity (Utility / Tool).

## Violence

- Cartoon / fantasy violence: **No**
- Realistic violence: **No**
- Violence toward real people or groups: **No**
- Blood / gore: **No**

## Sexuality

- Sexual content: **No**
- Nudity: **No**

## Language

- Profanity / crude humor: **No**

## Controlled substance

- References to drugs, alcohol, or tobacco: **No**

## Simulated gambling

- Simulated gambling: **No**
- Real-money gambling: **No**

## User-generated content / social features

- User-generated content shared with other users: **No**
  *(Everything the user imports stays on their device. There is no feed,
  profile, chat, or upload to a shared service.)*
- Unmoderated user-to-user chat: **No**
- User location shared with other users: **No**

## Miscellaneous

- Personal information collected: **No** (see data-safety.md)
- Digital purchases: **No**
- Advertising: **No by default.** The open-source build has ads disabled
  (`ADS_ENABLED = false`). Any store build that turns ads on must update
  this answer to "Yes — ads shown" and disclose AdMob.
